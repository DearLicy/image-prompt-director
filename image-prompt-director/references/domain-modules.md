# Domain Modules

Use the relevant module to add image-type-specific anchors.

## Article Cover / Editorial Poster

Include:

- source content summary, core theme, category, key symbols
- one dominant visual metaphor tied to the article
- horizontal or vertical cover ratio, text-safe area, crop safety
- editorial hierarchy: title area, subtitle area, metadata or badge area
- controlled background detail, not generic scenery
- commercial-magazine or documentary-poster lighting

Avoid:

- unrelated people, unrelated skyline, random technology particles
- fake facts not present in source material
- illegible Chinese text, repeated typography, heavy glow, cheap template effects

## Magazine Cover

Include:

- cover identity: fashion, culture, tech, game, luxury, youth, business
- subject overlap with masthead or title layers if desired
- dense but gridded typography, wide letter spacing, small issue details
- one strong face/object focus and a clean editorial background
- print-like contrast, premium paper or glossy finish cues

Avoid:

- too many text blocks
- random fake brand names
- decorative clutter that hides the subject

## Portrait / Fashion / Cosplay

Include:

- adult subject when glamour styling is requested
- face, expression, gaze direction, pose, hand behavior
- hair physics, fabric structure, skin texture, makeup style
- lens and aperture: 50mm/85mm/100mm, shallow depth of field
- key light, catchlight, rim light, background falloff
- costume fidelity and material translation for cosplay

Avoid:

- plastic skin, over-smoothed face, empty gaze, broken fingers
- impossible waist/neck/shoulder anatomy
- melted fabric, cheap costume material, fake wig look unless intentionally stylized
- explicit body-part fixation when the desired outcome is editorial or premium

## Product / Commercial Hero Image

Include:

- product category, exact shape, surface material, label behavior
- hero angle: three-quarter, front-on, macro detail, exploded view, floating packshot
- studio lighting: large softbox, edge highlights, reflection control
- background: seamless, gradient, lifestyle scene, shelf, countertop, outdoor context
- scale cues, props that communicate use case
- e-commerce or advertising crop requirements

Avoid:

- distorted logos, unreadable labels, extra product copies, warped packaging
- cluttered props stealing attention

## Architecture / Interior

Include:

- building/interior type, era, material palette, lighting time
- camera height, focal length, one-point/two-point perspective
- human scale if useful
- weather, landscaping, furniture, spatial flow
- natural light path and shadow geometry

Avoid:

- impossible stairs/windows, warped columns, inconsistent perspective
- excessive decorative objects that break spatial clarity

## Food / Beverage

Include:

- dish identity, ingredients, plating, steam, gloss, crumbs, condensation
- camera angle: top-down, 45-degree table view, macro
- light: window side light, restaurant practicals, soft editorial food lighting
- surface: ceramic plate, linen, wood grain, stainless steel, glass
- appetite cues: fresh cut, sauce sheen, texture contrast

Avoid:

- plastic food, impossible ingredients, messy plating unless requested

## Landscape / Travel / Nature

Include:

- location type, season, weather, time of day
- foreground/midground/background layers
- lens: wide angle, telephoto compression, aerial, macro nature
- atmosphere: mist, dust, rain, snow, haze, water reflection
- scale anchor: path, person silhouette, vehicle, structure if needed

Avoid:

- generic postcard look, oversaturated HDR, random landmark mixing

## Anime / Illustration / Concept Art

Include:

- style family: anime key visual, manga cover, painterly concept art, cel-shaded, watercolor, ink
- character design, silhouette, costume shape language, expression
- action line, camera angle, background story
- color script, rim light, atmospheric depth
- rendering finish: clean line art, painterly brush, flat vector, high-detail splash art

Avoid:

- mixed incompatible styles unless intentionally hybrid
- overdesigned details that make the character unreadable

## Game Asset / Sprite / Item Icon

Include:

- asset type: sprite sheet, isometric tile, inventory icon, card art, UI button, 3D prop
- view angle, silhouette readability, transparent background if needed
- scale and padding, consistent lighting, outline behavior
- animation frames or state list if applicable
- export intent: PNG, square icon, atlas-ready, orthographic

Avoid:

- cropped edges, inconsistent scale, noisy background, tiny unreadable details

## Logo / Icon / Brand Mark

Include:

- brand name only if provided and exact text is needed
- symbol idea, geometry, negative space, line weight, color limit
- flat vector style, high contrast, scalable silhouette
- lockup preference: icon only, wordmark, horizontal, badge
- background: transparent, white, dark, or mockup

Avoid:

- photorealism unless making a logo mockup
- too many colors, gradients, complex small details, fake text

## Infographic / Diagram

Include:

- data or concept hierarchy, exact labels if needed
- layout: flowchart, timeline, matrix, map, dashboard, exploded diagram
- typography scale, spacing, icon style, color coding
- text-safe simplicity; prefer fewer labels

Avoid:

- dense paragraph text, tiny labels, invented numbers, unreadable charts

## UI Mockup / App Screen

Include:

- platform: iOS, Android, web, desktop, kiosk
- screen purpose, primary workflow, component hierarchy
- visual system: typography, density, color, icon style
- device frame or no frame, realistic content density
- states: empty, loading, success, error if requested

Avoid:

- placeholder lorem ipsum unless requested
- impossible UI alignment, tiny unreadable controls, decorative clutter
